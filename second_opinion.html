Frequently Asked Questions

A second opinion service involves seeking the expertise of another medical professional to validate or provide additional insights into a diagnosis or treatment. It adds an extra layer of confidence and ensures informed decision-making about your healthcare.

You can register for our second opinion service by filling out our online form with your medical details, uploading relevant reports, and selecting your preferred consultation method. Our team will then match you with the appropriate specialist.

We maintain strict confidentiality protocols and comply with international medical privacy standards. All consultations are conducted through secure platforms, and your medical information is only shared with authorized specialists involved in your case.

Our medical team reviews your case details and matches you with specialists based on your specific condition, the complexity of your case, and the specialist's expertise and experience in treating similar conditions.

You'll need to provide your current medical reports, test results, imaging studies (X-rays, CT scans, MRIs), medication history, and a detailed description of your symptoms and medical history.
