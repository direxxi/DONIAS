About Donias

Nigeria's Premier Medical Tourism Agency

Donias Medical Tourism is Nigeria's leading medical tourism agency, dedicated to connecting patients with world-class, affordable medical facilities across the globe.

Through our personalized service approach and strategic partnerships with internationally acclaimed medical facilities in India, UAE, Germany, the United Kingdom, and Israel, we ensure every client receives exceptional care and achieves optimal health outcomes.

We don't just arrange medical travel—we create life-changing healthcare experiences.

What We Offer

Comprehensive Services
For Your Journey

Free Second Opinion

Make informed healthcare decisions with confidence. We provide complimentary second opinions from our distinguished panel of international medical experts, ensuring you receive the best possible guidance for your treatment options.

Virtual Expert Consultations

Connect with world-class specialists from the comfort of your home. Our personalized video consultations allow you to meet your doctors, build trust, and address all your concerns well before your travel date.

Complete Visa Support

Navigate visa complexities with ease. Our experienced team manages the entire process—from documentation preparation to approval—allowing you to focus solely on your upcoming treatment with complete peace of mind.

Full Travel & Concierge

Experience white-glove service from arrival to departure. We arrange airport transfers, comfortable accommodations, local transportation, translation services, and personalized dining—ensuring your complete comfort throughout your stay.

Hospital Coordination

Focus entirely on your recovery while we handle all administrative details. From hospital check-ins and admission procedures to post-discharge follow-ups, we manage every formality so you can heal without worry.

Recovery & Wellness Tours

Celebrate your healing journey with curated experiences. Once cleared by your medical team, enjoy personalized cultural tours and relaxation activities that help you rejuvenate while experiencing the beauty of your destination.
