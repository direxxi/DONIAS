About Us

Nigeria's Top Medical Tourism Agency

Donias Medical Tourism is a Nigeria based medical tourism agency that connects Nigerian patients with quality and affordable medical facilities abroad.

We ensure a rewarding and positive outcome to our clients through our personalized service and partnership with some of the world's leading medical facilities in India, UAE, Germany, the United Kingdom and Israel.

Our Unique Offerings

Free Second Opinion

When dealing with medical issues, consulting with another doctor can be an important step to finding best care. That is why we provide patients with free second opinion from our expert panel of doctors.

Talk To Our Experts From Anywhere

We offer personalized video consultations with our expert consultants, giving you the chance to connect with your doctors, build trust, and get all your questions answered—well before you travel.

Visa Assistance

Visa processing can be complicated, but our dedicated assistants handle everything—from documentation to approvals—so you can focus on preparing for your treatment with complete peace of mind.

Airport Shuttle / Conceirge Services

We go beyond airport shuttles to offer full concierge services, ensuring your comfort and catering to your every need throughout your stay.

Logistics Of Your Stay

We provide all-round logistics to give patients a memorable stay including assisting you to find affordable hotel or guest house tailored to your needs, foods, translator etc.

Hospital Arrangements

To ensure your energy is focused entirely on getting better, we take care of all the formalities—check-ins, hospital admission, and even post-discharge follow-ups—so you can heal without worry.

Post Treatment Tours

If you wish, we can arrange personalized tours after your treatment—so once you’re feeling your best, you can relax and experience the sights and sounds of the region."
