Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our visa services

How long does it take to prepare my application? ‚ñº

Our average preparation time is 48-72 hours for Standard and Premium packages, depending on document availability and completeness. Basic package users get immediate access to checklists and templates.

What funds or proof of ties are typically required? ‚ñº

Requirements vary by destination, but generally include 3-6 months of bank statements showing consistent income/savings, employment letter, property ownership, family ties, and return ticket bookings. We provide specific guidance based on your destination during the eligibility check.

Do you guarantee visa approval? ‚ñº

No one can guarantee visa approval as the final decision rests with the embassy or consulate. However, we focus on submitting complete, accurate, and strong applications that meet all official requirements, which significantly improves your chances. Our 92% success rate speaks to our thoroughness.

Can you book my appointment and biometrics? ‚ñº

Yes! All Standard and Premium packages include appointment booking service. We monitor availability, secure your slot, and send you all necessary confirmation details and preparation instructions.

What happens if my application is refused? ‚ñº

Premium package clients receive appeal and reapplication guidance at no extra cost. For other packages, we offer discounted reapplication support. We'll review the refusal reasons and help strengthen your case for the next attempt.

Are government visa fees included in your pricing? ‚ñº

No, our service fees cover our professional assistance only. Government visa application fees are paid directly to the embassy/consulate and vary by destination (typically $100-$200 USD equivalent). We provide a transparent breakdown of all costs upfront.

How do you keep my data secure? ‚ñº

We use bank-level encryption (SSL/TLS) for all data transmission and storage. Your documents are stored on secure servers with restricted access. We comply with NDPR (Nigeria Data Protection Regulation) and GDPR standards. Data is retained only as long as necessary and can be deleted upon request.

Do you offer refunds if I change my mind? ‚ñº

Yes, we offer full refunds if you cancel before we begin work on your file. Once document preparation has started, partial refunds apply based on work completed. No refunds are possible after submission. See our full refund policy in the Terms & Conditions.
