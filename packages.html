Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our packages

What's included in the package price? ‚ñº

All our packages include accommodation, daily breakfast, visa processing support, airport transfers, specified tours and activities, and travel insurance. International flights can be added upon request. Each package details specific inclusions.

How do I book a package? ‚ñº

Booking is easy! Call us at +234 803 716 2368, email tourism@donias.com.ng, or WhatsApp us. We'll discuss your preferences, confirm availability, and guide you through the payment process. A deposit secures your booking.

Can packages be customized? ‚ñº

Absolutely! All packages can be tailored to your preferences. Want to extend your stay, upgrade hotels, add activities, or visit additional cities? Just let us know and we'll create a custom itinerary for you.

What about visa processing? ‚ñº

We handle all visa applications! Our experienced team will guide you through documentation, complete application forms, and liaise with embassies on your behalf. Visa fees are included in most packages (check individual package details).

What's your cancellation policy? ‚ñº

Cancellations more than 60 days before departure: Full refund minus 10% admin fee. 30-60 days: 50% refund. Less than 30 days: No refund, but you can transfer to another traveler or reschedule (subject to availability). Travel insurance is recommended.
